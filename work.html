Work

Currently I work at Mapbox, a mapping platform for developers. In my present role I focus on Business Development - identifying new market opportunities for product adoption. Prior to Mapbox I was employed by ESRI; I acted as an internal consultant making recommendations at the executive level regarding strategy, market analysis, product competition, and product development for the ArcGIS Platform. Before my work in the technology sector I focused on health and public policy analysis.

Last year, I co-founded two non profits, Economic Research Center and Reharvest Food Source Inc.

Check back in 2018 to see the exciting work soon to come from Reharvest!
